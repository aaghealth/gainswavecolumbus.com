About {{site.physician_name}}

Dr. Erickson attended the Uniformed Services University of the Health Sciences--F. Edward Hebert School of Medicine-- after completing almost eight years of active duty as an enlisted member of the United States Navy. She subsequently completed a combined residency and fellowship program in Psychiatry, Pediatrics and Child and Adolescent Psychiatry. She maintains her Board Certification in Psychiatry and has also completed Board Certification in Pediatrics. She has also practiced Emergency Medicine in the past for about ten years on a part-time basis.
