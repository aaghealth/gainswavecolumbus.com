How Does it Work?

The science behind GAINSWave has been developed in Europe for decades. What scientists have found is that when acoustic waves are introduced to a person’s skin, it will transfer wave energy into the recipients soft tissue. This energy is full of healing and growth factors that stimulate the remediation of many injuries, illnesses and conditions.

In cases of erectile dysfunction, the soundwaves work to create the processes of Angiogenesis, the healing and growth of existing blood vessels, and Neovascularization, the generation of new blood vessels. This helps to treat the most common form of ED - vasculogenic erectile dysfunction - which is caused by low blood flow to the penis. Without a sufficient and sustained flow of blood, the penis will not lengthen, expand or achieve the necessary rigidity for sexual intercourse. By expanding existing blood vessels and creating new ones, GAINSWave improves blood flow to the patient’s penis, which is how scientists in Europe have supported its effectiveness as a sexual performance enhancement treatment.
